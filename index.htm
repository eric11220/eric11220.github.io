Kuan-Ying (Eric) Lee

Ph.D Candidate, CS, UIUC

I am a Ph.D student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where I am co-advised by Klara Nahrstedt and Yuxiong Wang. My research focuses on multi-view content analysis and delivery, including, but not limited to, multi-camera view recommendation and virtual conferencing with 360° videos.

Before coming to UIUC, I received a M.S. and B.S. in Computer Science at National Taiwan University, where I was advised by Winston Hsu.
